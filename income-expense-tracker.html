Income Expense Tracker

Tracking your personal income and expense is the best way to know exactly where you money is coming from and going to. This way you'll be able to create financial awareness. Knowing where your money is going and how you spend them will help you know what habits you need to change in order to at least balance your lifestyle to achieve financial goals you want in life.

This tracking software is designd to help you ease out in tracking your income and expenses. All you have to do is input all the sources of your income and expenses it will automatically calculate the total result for you.

Income

If you are an employee, enter the Monthly Gross Income you received from your main job plus income from side jobs or investments

It is highly recommended you use the (INCOME - SAVINGS = EXPENSES) formula
