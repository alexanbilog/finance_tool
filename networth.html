Glossary of Terms
Real Properties

Your real property includes home (detached or semi-detached) and condo unit or apartment that you own. Includes in the others some real real estate properties like rental property, cottage, etc. Enter the estimated current total value.

Personal Properties
Personal Property is movable property. It is anything that can be subject to ownership except land. Examples of personal property includes your car, appliances, furniture, boats and collectibles.
Investments/Insurance
Include the current account value of any life insurance you have. Term insurance is rarely considered an asset. Your investments may include bonds, stocks, Mutual Funds, UITF..
Liquid Assets
A liquid assets is cash on hand or any assets that can be readily converted into cash like saving account, checking account, Money Market Funds and my include cash allocated for emergency fund.
Glossary of Terms
Real Property Loans
The current total balance of any real estate loans you made. For example, the remaining balance that you must pay for a home loan.
Personal Loans
Any current total balance of all loans you made to purchase personal assets like vehicles, furnitures, clothings, etc.
Credit Card Balance
The current total unpaid credit balance of all your credit card accounts.
Other Liabilities
All liabilities that are not included above such as business loans, friends loans, Social Security loans and others.
