SSS Pension Calculator

Do you know how much pension you will recieve from the Social Security System or SSS when you retire? This calculator will help you out to have an idea on how much pension you could recieve from SSS.

Your monthly pension depends of which formula yields the highest.
Take note though, that if your CYS is less than 10 years, you will recieve your pension through lump sum.

Formula 1

P1,200 for members with at least 10 CYS and P2,400 for members with at least twenty and above CYS.

₱
Formula 2

40 % of your Average Monthly Salary Credit or AMSC.

₱
Formula 3

P300 + 20% of AMSC + [(2% of AMSC) X (CYS - 10)]

₱

Disclaimer: This calculator is for illustrative purposes only and does not reflect the actual SSS pension that you will get. It does not take into account the exact AMSC you made. For more details on SSS programs, members can drop by the nearest SSS branch, visit the SSS web site www.sss.gov.ph.
